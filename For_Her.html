Pour Femme

The Pour Femme Collection introduces a range of opulent fine fragrances for the modern woman who embodies confidence, elegance, and grace. Each fragrance of the collection is invigorating yet elegant, blended explicitly for the NEESH Woman, who is bold, charismatic, and uncompromising.

Packed in the feminine iteration of the NEESH's famed emerald-cute shards, the Pour Femme collection features a range of fragrances from floral, vibrant, sultry to even aphrodisiac – all blended to capture the essence of bold luxury.

Luxe Collection

Introducing the Luxe Collection—a series of regal fragrances centered on the enchanting note of black rose. Each scent is meticulously crafted to capture the opulent, royal essence of the rose, transporting you to a realm of glamour and luxury with every spritz. The collection's distinctive design features matte black shards with glossy metal accents on the cap and plaque. Explore Luxe Hayat, Luxe Aoud, and Luxe Mirage, each a testament to timeless elegance.

Bureau Collection

The Bureau Collection is an elegant range of luxury perfumes crafted for modern professionals seeking a sophisticated, confident scent that exudes opulence. Each fragrance blends airy, refreshing notes with rare, captivating accents for a bold character and lasting appeal. The collection features distinctive transparent shards with matte metal work on the cap and plaque. Discover the allure of Glazed Water, Signature Scent, and Cologne Sophistique..

Haute Collection

The Haute Collection showcases luxurious perfumes, each centered on a legendary note enhanced by carefully chosen accents that elevate the scent to unforgettable heights. Launching with Haute Tobacco, this curated series will soon expand with Haute Vetiver and Haute Wood.
